Frequently Asked Questions

How does VELORA use AI in the design process?

Our AI analyzes thousands of design parameters including room dimensions, lighting conditions, and your personal style preferences to generate optimal layout suggestions. It then collaborates with our human designers who refine these concepts into bespoke solutions.

Can AI really understand my personal style?

Through our style quiz and image recognition technology, our AI identifies patterns in your preferences with remarkable accuracy. It cross-references these with current trends and timeless design principles to suggest options you're likely to love.

How accurate are the AI-generated 3D renderings?

Our photorealistic renderings have a 92% accuracy rate for color, texture, and spatial relationships. We always provide physical samples of key materials before finalizing any design to ensure complete satisfaction.

Does AI replace human designers at VELORA?

Not at all. Our AI serves as a powerful tool that enhances our designers' capabilities, allowing them to explore more options and focus on creative nuances while the AI handles data analysis and repetitive tasks.
